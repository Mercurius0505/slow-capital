01 / WHY
The honest case

Most beginners lose money. The reasons are predictable.

74–89%

of retail CFD accounts lose money, according to ESMA. Most retail traders fund a live account on day one, copy a strategy from YouTube, ignore position sizing, and blow up within three months. This is not a personality problem. It is a sequencing problem.

01

They start with real money.

Demo accounts replicate market conditions at zero financial cost. Skipping demo means paying tuition with real capital — and the tuition is brutal.

02

They trade strategies before risk rules.

Position sizing, stop-loss discipline, and the 1% rule decide outcomes. Strategy decides which trades — but risk rules decide whether you survive long enough for strategy to matter.

03

They learn from people selling them something.

Most "free" courses are funnels for €2,000 trading rooms. The incentive is enrollment, not your performance. We have nothing to upsell.
